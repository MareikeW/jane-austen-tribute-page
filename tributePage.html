Jane Austen

English author 1775-1817

Life

Jane Austen was born on 16th December 1775 in Steventon, England.
Her father was a clergyman and her mother stemmed from a wealthy family.
Early on in her life Jane Austen had an interest in literature.
She would write poems, stories and even plays for herself or for entertaining her family.
In her late teens or early twenties she began writing full-length novels,
the first of which was "Sense and Sensibiliy" which wasn't published until 1811.
During Jane Austen's lifetime she published four novels in her lifetime.
Another three of her novels were published after her death in 1817.

Works
